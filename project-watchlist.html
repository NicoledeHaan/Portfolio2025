Movies watchlist

My boyfriend and I love watching movies and series together, but managing our lists across different streaming services like Netflix, HBO Max, and Hayu is tough. We want to create our own platform to keep everything in one place so we can easily manage and save all the shows we want to watch. Additionally, we want to stay updated when a new season of a show is released, as we currently find out quite late.

Service

Research, Webdesign, Branding

Client

Own fun project

Tools

Adobe XD

How can me and my boyfriend easily manage and save all the movies and series we want to watch together in our own platform?

Add films to your watchlist

Films and series can be added to your watchlist from any streaming service with just one click. All your favorites are conveniently organized in one place, making it easy to keep track of everything you want to watch.

View film details

Comprehensive information about movies and series is available, including trailers and reviews. Based on our preferences, the platform highlights and prioritizes content that matches our interests.

Stay up to date

Receive automatic notifications when a new season of a watched show is released, eliminating the need for manual searches. Stay informed about your favorite series effortlessly.
