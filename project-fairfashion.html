Fair Fashion app

Our project for HvA focused on getting students away from buying fast fashion and start buying sustainable fashion. We designed features that help users to chose eco friendly options that are also fashionable and educate users on their positive environmental impact.

Service

Research, prototyping, UX Design, UI Design

Client

HvA

Tools

Adobe XD

"How might we motivate students aged 20-25 to buy sustainable fashion through an online application?"

Style test

Our style test helps you find sustainable fashion that fits your taste. Answer a few questions and get personalized, eco-friendly clothing recommendations—making conscious shopping effortless.

Favorites of others

The favorites feature makes finding social approved sustainable fashion easier. See what other users love and discover stylish, eco-friendly clothing that matches your taste.

Sustainability insights

Broaden your knowledge about sustainability. See how sustainably the clothing is made and what you contribute to a sustainable world by purchasing a piece of clothing.
