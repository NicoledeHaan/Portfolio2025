AI Buddy app

Our project for the Netherlands Cancer Institute and Amsterdam Fashion Institute helps prostate cancer survivors stay active. Exercise fights fatigue and can improve their ability to undergo treatment. We created an AI wearable and app to motivate and track progress daily, supporting their journey to better health.

Service

Research, prototyping, UX Design, UI Design

Client

Netherlands Cancer Institute &
Amsterdam Fashion Institute

Tools

Adobe XD

How might we encourage prostate cancer survivors to be (more) physically active through wearables?

View your daily improvement

Prostate cancer patients can track their daily exercise, including steps, calories burned, distance, and heart rate. The app provides valuable insights to help manage health and keeps healthcare providers updated with accurate data, supporting ongoing treatment.

Detailed insights

The app provides detailed insights into their daily physical activity, including steps taken, calories burned, distance covered, and heart rate.

Motivate each other

Prostate cancer clients often feel isolated, making it hard to discuss with family and friends. Now, connect with peers for mutual support and motivation. Track goals and send personalized messages through our wearable, building encouragement and friendships in your recovery journey.
