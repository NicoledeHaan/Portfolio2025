Partnerplan webpage

MediaLab, a growing file-sharing company, wanted to focus on collaborations and attracting potential clients. To do this, I designed a page where potential clients could find detailed information about MediaLab's services and benefits of partnering with them.

Service

Webdesign

Client

MediaLab

Tools

Adobe XD

How can MediaLab provide potential collaborations and clients with information about their services and benefits of a collaboration, without back and forth communication?

What’s in it for you?

By describing directly and concisely why you should become partners with medialab and what's in it for you, potential partners know at a glance if this is something for them.

Meet MediaLab

By showing a short and concise video, clients are instantly introduced to MediaLab and immediately know what medialab is and does.

Gain trust

Showing potential clients the position MediaLab holds in the Dutch market strengthens confidence in the company and its services.
