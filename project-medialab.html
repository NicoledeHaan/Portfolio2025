Shared folders app

MediaLab, a file-sharing company, faced a challenge where clients could only share single files, causing frustration. To address this, I designed a feature enabling folder sharing and facilitating direct communication for approvals and feedback on videos and photos within the tool.

Service

Research, prototyping, UX Design, UI Design

Client

MediaLab.co, MediaLab Online

Tools

Adobe XD

How can MediaLab customers keep communication with their clients about the shared folders within 1 tool, so they can have direct contact whether the files are approved or need to be modified?

Share MediaLab folders

This new feature from MediaLab allows you to share entire folders, multiple folders, and subfolders with just one click. It streamlines the sharing process, saving time and makes the process more efficient by eliminating the need to send individual files separately.

Receive & approve files

A MediaLab app to enable users and their clients to communicate directly about shared files. Clients can now easily approve or suggest changes to files, streamlining the collaboration process. This integrated approach keeps everything in one place, removing the need for additional tools.

See approvals & feedback

This new feature in the MediaLab tool has been enhanced to send users notifications of client approvals or changes, including details about who made them and when, especially when shared with multiple people.
